National Antimicrobial Guidelines (NAGS)

About NAGS

The National Antimicrobial Guidelines (NAGS) provide evidence-based recommendations for the appropriate use of antimicrobial agents to combat infections and reduce antimicrobial resistance.

These guidelines are designed to support healthcare professionals in making informed decisions about antimicrobial therapy.
