About P&C Tutorials

The Patient & Community Tutorials (P&Cs) is part of the problem based curriculum in the University of Papua New Guinea's MBBS Degree Program.

The information is from 2001 and has not been updated since.

This project is a labour of love that aims to transfer the information from an unnoficial PDF source onto a website.

It is unoffical and not supported by the university in any way.
